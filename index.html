About Eloheh

Welcome to Eloheh, a reputable brand in creative and caring medical care. By providing individualized care and high-quality medical supplies, we are dedicated to enhancing the health of both individuals and communities.

Our goal is to advance holistic health solutions by fusing the strength of preventive care, sustainability, and accessibility with contemporary medical advancements.

Our Story

Eloheh was founded on the belief that health is not just the absence of illness but a state of physical, mental, and spiritual well-being. Inspired by the meaning of "Eloheh"—a word rooted in harmony and wholeness—we embarked on a journey to create a company where care comes first.

Starting as a small team of passionate healthcare professionals and community advocates, Eloheh launched with just a handful of wellness products and mobile health services in underserved areas. Today, we’ve grown into a full-spectrum provider, offering:

  • Natural and sustainable health products
  • Telemedicine and in-person consultations
  • Health education programs
  • Community-based wellness initiatives

Our History

  • 2018 – Founded Eloheh with a mission of delivering community-focused, whole-person care
  • 2019 – Launching our first product line: natural herbal health products
  • 2020 – Expansion into telehealth and mobile clinics at the height of the COVID-19 pandemic
  • 2022 – Launched Eloheh Health Hub app, which brought thousands to virtual health care
  • 2024 – Opened Chicago, IL flagship well-being center
  • Today – Caring for thousands of patients nationwide with holistic health services
